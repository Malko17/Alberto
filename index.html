About Me

I am a Research Lecturer in Applied Mathematics at the University of Yaound√© I, specializing in mathematical modelling with prediction through dynamical systems theory, chaos theory, and bifurcation theory.

My research spans from asymptotic analysis of ordinary and partial differential equations to coupled systems modeling problems in epidemiology and ecosystems. Currently exploring stochastic approaches and fractional order derivatives in mathematical modeling.

Born on January 16, 1989, I hold a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from the University of Maroua (2022) with First Class Honours, focusing on the mathematical modeling and control of vector-borne diseases.
